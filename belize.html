Median Age

In Belize, the median age for men is 23 years whereas the median age for women is 24.8 years. The total median age is 23.9 years of age.

Introduction

In 1862, Belize was declared the colony of British Honduras. Belize finally became an independent country in 1981. Due to on-going contraversy between Guatemala and Belize, they have resorted to the ICJ to settle their differences.

Prominent Issues in Belize
